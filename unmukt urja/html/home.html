Who are we?


We are a group of companies focused on creating alternative solutions to meet domestic & industrial requirements of fuel from biomass residue. Our VISION is to provide alternative solutions to meet our daily needs by creating localised circular solutions. And our MISSION is to replace Coal, Natural Gas, Urea, Carbon Dioxide and Crude Oil with products derived from Biomass Residue

“We are a triple bottom-line company having equal focus on profit, planet and people.”

Unnmukt is a group of companies constituting of Unnmukt Solutions Private limited (USPL) and Unnmukt Urja Private Limited (UUPL).
USPL was incorporated on February 2, 2022, to focus upon production of Biomass Pellets and Producer Gas to substitute coal in industrial application such as coal-based thermal power plants and steel plants.
UUPL was incorporated on October 16, 2019, to focus on production of CNG/ LNG from Biomass residue and Hydrothermal Liquefied Crude from Biomass residue.

What

do we do?


Biomass Pellet


Biomass pellets are our first product being produced for coal based thermal plants based on the regulatory requirement established by Ministry of Power, Government of India, through its letter number- 11/86/2017-Th.II dated October 8, 2021. Comparing the end-to-end cost with coal, Biomass pellets are 59% less expensive than coal.

R-CNG

(Renewable Compressed Natural Gas)
&

R- LNG

(Renewable Liquefied Natural Gas)

R-CNG or L- CNG can substitute transportation and heating requirements for both, industrial and domestic applications. UUPL has received an LOI from a leading Public Sector Unit for 10 years. Production shall commence by end of Financial Year 2023. By-products of R-CNG or R-LNG production from biomass residue shall also be commercialized by the bank such as Carbon Dioxide and Nitrogen-enriched Phosphorusrich organic manure.
